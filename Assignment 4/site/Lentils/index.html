LENTILS / دال

Our farmers wait right till the month of May to harvest the golden grains of Lentils. Then these grains go through several industrial processes in which we do stacking, handling, threshing, cleaning, and hauling. After that they are packed in world class packaging facilities so that nothing could harm the product in it's further journey. At last they are stored in our sophisticated warehouses where our team is always making sure to maintain a healthy environment for the product.
Types of Lentils include the following:

Types can be classified according to their size, whether they are split or whole, or shelled or unshelled. Seed coats can range from light green to deep purple, as well as being tan, grey, brown, black or mottled. Shelled lentils show the colour of the cotyledon which can be yellow, orange, red, or green.

Yellow Leentils

Small split lentils with a tendency to break down during cooking. Great for making thick soups/dal.

Brown Leentils
