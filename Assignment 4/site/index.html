WHY R&M ARE DIFFERENT FROM ALL

The founding father of the corporation, Saith Rasheed Bhai stepped into the business as a broker in the early 90s. Due to his hard work and ambition he soon earned prominence in the market and established "Rice and Millet". With the right leadership "Rice and Millet" found its way in the imports/exports sector. With the passage of time "Rice and Millet" kept growing and in order to meet the requirements of the clients "Riceand Millet" also expanded its supply base in Punjab. Today, we deal in almost all sort of agricultural products among which the highlights are rice, wheat, chickpeas, Millet etc.
Our goal is to expand our products in the Western as well as the Central Asian markets under and we are pretty capable to do so as we offer high quality products with very competitive prices.
Our customer care service is best of it's kind. Which include, consignment tracking, online payments facility, sample testing etc. We offer deliveries of goods all across the globe. Our representatives are always there to assist our clients. And our goal is to satisfy our clients.
As we are known for our quality and customer dealing we aim to maintain not to compromise policy on our standard as set and practiced by our elders which were our key to success. Our mission\goal is to spread our roots to all over the globe by more imports and exports and by spreading our network in near future InshaAllah.
